## Search/content issues
- **Weak positions:** Several high-volume and high-intent keywords (e.g., "fusion adalah", "biaya sekolah chef", "auguste escoffier") are ranking in positions 2-7, indicating potential for improvement to position 1.
- **Thin footprint:** With 226 organic keywords and 853 organic traffic, the site has a relatively small search presence for an educational institution, suggesting limited content depth or breadth.
- **Keyword gaps:** While ranking for definitional terms and chef roles, there appear to be gaps in content targeting specific course offerings, career paths, alumni success stories, or location-specific searches relevant to a culinary institute.
