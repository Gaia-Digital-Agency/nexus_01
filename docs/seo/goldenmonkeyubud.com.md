## Search/content issues
- **Thin footprint:** The site ranks for a very limited number of organic keywords (27), indicating a narrow presence in search results.
- **High brand dependency:** Over 98% of organic traffic comes from the branded term "golden monkey ubud," suggesting minimal non-branded visibility.
- **Weak positions for relevant terms:** Several relevant keywords like "all you can eat ubud" (Pos 8), "high tea bali" (Pos 27), "best things to do in ubud" (Pos 78), and "must visit places in ubud" (Pos 65, 71) are ranking outside the top 3 or even top 10, indicating missed opportunities for broader reach.
- **Keyword gaps:** Opportunity to create content around related local attractions and dining experiences beyond core Chinese food, as suggested by keywords like "best things to do in ubud" and "must visit places in ubud."
